ICTvanafMorgen

I have worked on creating a roster app using Symfony with ICTvanafMorgen.

Schedule Roster

In the app you can create a schedule for the employees, this is on a week by week basis.

Daily attendance registration

One of the main features is able to register the status of any scheduled employee on a number of statussen, this only useable on the day itself.

Weekly attendance registration

If one wants to change the registration on other days one can change this in a week overvieuw.

Profiel overvieuw

Employees can verify their schedules and confirm the accuracy of the registrations.

Reporting

The owner can see all registrations and can easily see non-attendance or other information.

Weekroster

A shareable weekroster where all scheduled people are shown which is downloadable in pdf
